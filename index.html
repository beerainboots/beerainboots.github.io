Recipe for Pancakes?!

Things you probably need include...

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsps melted butter
  • 3 1/2 tsps baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Make a well in center of mix, add liquid ingredients.
  3. Find griddle or pan. Lightly oil.
  4. Locate stove.
  5. Use medium high heat.
  6. Make pancakes (1/4 cup each).
  7. Eat pancakes!
